命名故事 Our Naming Story

Cerise Select 的命名源於我們對卓越品質的承諾。 「Cerise」是法語中「櫻桃」的意思，象徵著我們初期專注於櫻桃這項高品質水果的嚴選精神。 我們希望透過這個名稱，傳達我們對產品來源、品質把關以及為消費者提供最佳選擇的堅持。 「Select」則代表我們精挑細選、嚴格篩選的理念，確保每一批水果都符合最高標準， 為全球供應商和消費者建立信任與信心。 The name Cerise Select originates from our commitment to exceptional quality. "Cerise" is the French word for "cherry," symbolizing our initial focus on the meticulous selection of this high-quality fruit. Through this name, we aim to convey our dedication to product sourcing, quality control, and providing consumers with the best choices. "Select" represents our philosophy of meticulous curation and strict screening, ensuring that every batch of fruit meets the highest standards, building trust and confidence with both global suppliers and consumers.
